# Complete Rotation System Breakdown

## Base Position Mapping (Rotation 1 - Serving)

When starting in **Rotation 1** and **serving**, the base positions are:
- **Position 1**: Setter (S)
- **Position 2**: Outside Hitter 1 (OH1)
- **Position 3**: Middle Blocker 1 (MB1)
- **Position 4**: Opposite (OPP)
- **Position 5**: Outside Hitter 2 (OH2)
- **Position 6**: Middle Blocker 2 (MB2)

## Libero Substitution Rules Summary

### When Libero Enters (Replaces MB):
1. **MB is in back row** (Positions 1, 5, or 6) **AND**
2. **Team is receiving** (not serving)

### When Libero Exits (MB Returns):
1. **MB rotates to front row** (Positions 2, 3, or 4)
2. **Team is serving** (MB stays even if in back row)

### Special Cases:
- **MB1 in Pos 1 when receiving**: Libero replaces MB1
- **MB2 in Pos 1 when receiving**: Libero replaces MB2
- **MB in Pos 5 or 6 when receiving**: Libero replaces MB
- **MB in front row (Pos 2, 3, or 4)**: Always shows MB (no Libero)
- **MB serving**: Always shows MB (no Libero, even if in back row)

---

## Rotation Progression

Rotations progress **counter-clockwise** when the team wins a point while receiving:
- Rotation 1 → Rotation 2 → Rotation 3 → Rotation 4 → Rotation 5 → Rotation 6 → Rotation 1 (cycle)

**Rotation changes occur when:**
- Team wins a point while **receiving**
- Rotation number increases by 1 (wraps to 1 after 6)

---

## Court Position Reference

**Back Row** (serving positions): 1, 5, 6
**Front Row** (net positions): 2, 3, 4

**Position Layout** (viewed from above, our side):
```
        4   3   2
        5   6   1
```

Where:
- **1** = Back Right (server position)
- **2** = Front Right
- **3** = Front Center
- **4** = Front Left
- **5** = Back Left
- **6** = Back Center

## Notes

1. **Setter Position**: The setter's position determines the rotation number (1-6)
2. **Counter-clockwise Movement**: Each rotation shift moves players one position counter-clockwise
3. **Libero Visibility**: Libero only appears when replacing MB in back row during receiving
4. **Front Row MB**: Middle blockers in front row (Pos 2, 3, 4) are never replaced by Libero
5. **Serving MB**: Middle blockers serving (in back row) are never replaced by Libero
